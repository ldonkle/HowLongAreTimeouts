How Long Are Timeouts?

Updated 10.22.2019

NFL


Timeouts are 2 minutes.
30 seconds if no commmercial break.

Halftime is 12 minutes.

NBA


Timeouts are 75 seconds.


Halftime is 15 minutes.

NHL


Timeouts are 2 minutes.
30 seconds if no commerical break.

Time between periods is 17 minutes.

NCAA Football


Timeouts are 90 seonds.


Halftime is 20 minutes.

NCAA Basketball


Timeouts are 60 seonds or 75 seconds, depending on the media agreement.


Halftime is 15 minutes.

Soccer


There are no timeouts in soccer.

Halftime is 15 minutes.
